Hoa Phat actualize the spirit of leading with quality?

  • 26/08/2019
  • 20:32
  • Admin
  • Company news

Through many ups and downs during nearly 3 decades of establishment and development, Hoa Phat Group has been the No. 1 steel manufacturer in Vietnam in terms of construction steel and steel pipes for many years. This is the result of seriously implementing "dedication to leading with quality", conquering customers with the best quality products.

At Hoa Phat units, every process from input to output is strictly controlled according to ISO quality management system such as ISO 9001: 2015 (Quality management system), ISO 17025: 2017 (General requirements for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories), ISO 14001: 2015 (Environmental management systems).

For example, with construction steel products - a key product of Hoa Phat Group, all stages of the steel production chain under the closed blast furnace technology are strictly controlled from input materials as iron ore, coke coal and other additives. Only cast iron batches that meet the requirements are put into embryo production through the oxygen blowing stage - the most advanced technology in Vietnam today. After that, square billet of different standards and grades of steel are transferred to the rolling line to produce quality steel bars and rods.

The finished product then goes through rigorous testing again, and only good quality products can be sold to customers. Especially, the exported products have met international quality standards such as JIS GS3505: 2004, JIS G3112: 2010 (Japan), BS 4449: 2005 (United Kingdom), ASTM A615 (United States) ...

 

In order to achieve such top quality, Hoa Phat focuses on investing in modern equipment lines, constantly improving, upgrading equipment, analyzing and checking the quality system with the most modern and advanced machines and equipment in the world. These include the world's largest suppliers of metallurgical and analytical technology equipment that have cooperated with Hoa Phat for decades, such as Danieli Group (Italy), SMS (Germany), INSTRON (USA). ), Shimadzu (Japan) ... At Hoa Phat Dung Quat Iron and Steel Integrated Complex, Hoa Phat has invested 2 buildings as an auto quality analysis zone for iron and steel and mechanical product testing area with a total value over 100 billion. The testing equipment, product analysis are annually tested/ calibrated to ensure that the test results always give the most accurate results.

 ABB Robot (Switzerland) at Quality Control Department - Hoa Phat Dung Quat Steel Company. This is a device to receive samples, automatically process samples of the most modern type today.

Especially, from the beginning of 2019, Hoa Phat Dung Quat Steel Joint Stock Company has officially put the overall management system of enterprise resources SAP ERP S / 4HANA into operation to manage all production and business activities at Hoa Phat Dung Quat iron and steel Integrated complex. This solution enables the company to collect real-time production and business data, control the quality and efficiency of production processes, so that the management can make decisions quickly. This management system will continue to be researched and deployed by the Group to other member companies.

Not only focusing on technology, equipment and management software, Hoa Phat also focuses on investing in training, improving skills for employees. Tens of thousands of personnel at member companies are periodically trained in various specialties to improve their professional skills, through cooperation with vocational colleges, professional centers and a number of local units. Newly graduated engineers, young and inexperienced staff will receive in-depth professional training at the factory, many opportunities for practical contact with equipment and machines, through many hours of careful practice before officially accepting quests. Besides, every year many training courses on 5S regulation in production, quality control, safe operation ... have taken place at Hoa Phat facilities across the country.

The investment in taking care of the highest quality of products has been thoroughly implemented by Hoa Phat Group to each member company. This activity will continue to be promoted in the coming time in order to maintain the reputation of Hoa Phat brand products to domestic and foreign customers, worthy of being a national brand, Top 10 construction materials enterprises. build the most prestigious, Top 10 most reputable listed companies, ...

Vuong Hang – Hong Hanh
