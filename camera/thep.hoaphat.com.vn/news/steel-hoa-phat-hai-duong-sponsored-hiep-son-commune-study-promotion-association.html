Steel Hoa Phat Hai Duong sponsored Hiep Son Commune Study Promotion Association

  • 06/10/2019
  • 20:31
  • Admin
  • Company news

In order to promote and well implement the study encouragement and talent promotion activities, on September 29, 2019, Hiep Son Commune Study Encouragement Association held a rewarding ceremony for teachers and students with outstanding achievements who achieved an award of national, provincial and district levels and poor students overcome difficulties. On this occasion, Hoa Phat Hai Duong Steel Company sponsored nearly 30 million VND to Hiep Son Commune Study Encouragement Association to award teachers and students at the commendation ceremony.

 

Attending were the Chairman of Kinh Mon Study Encouragement Association, President of Hiep Son Study Encouragement Association - Mr. Nguyen Thang Do, Chairman of Hiep Son Commune People's Committee - Mr. Tran Van Chuong, Secretary of Hiep Son Commune - Mr. Tran Van Co, Dai representatives of departments and teachers and students won the 2018-2019 school year. On the side of the company were Mr. Dong Xuan Van, Head of Electrical Equipment Department, Ms. Nong Hong Thom, Deputy Head of Organization Department, Hoa Phat Hai Duong Steel Joint Stock Company.

Accordingly, the study encouragement reward is given to the pupils who are children of Hiep Son commune, who attend preschools, primary schools, and junior high schools to achieve excellent student results from district, provincial and national levels and teachers won district-level good teachers. A total of 96 teachers and students were awarded.

This activity aims to cultivate and honor the studious examples and the movement of well teaching - learning in Hiep Son commune; Encourage teachers and students to constantly strive to achieve high results in teaching and learning. In addition, support poor students to excel in learning and individuals with achievements in the study promotion.

This is a meaningful activity of Hoa Phat Hai Duong Steel, practically celebrating the Vietnam Study Encouragement Day, contributing to the establishment of a study encouragement fund and care for the cause of growing people and improving the quality of training in the locality.

Hong Thom
